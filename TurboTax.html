Frequently Asked Questions

What is TurboTax?

TurboTax is an online tax‑preparation product from Intuit that helps users calculate deductions, complete forms, and file federal and state tax returns.

Is TurboTax better than H&R Block?

TurboTax is often preferred for its guided Q&A style and stronger support for side‑income and investments, while H&R Block is popular with users who like in‑person help or slightly different pricing.

Who should use FreeTaxUSA?

FreeTaxUSA is best for budget‑focused users comfortable with a more DIY experience and fewer guided steps.

Can freelancers use TurboTax?

Yes. TurboTax supports self‑employed filers, contractors, gig workers, and investors with tools for Schedule C, deductions, mileage, and more.
